Schedule (launchd window + random interval)

from to (exclusive)
launchd ticks every 10 min, but the wrapper exits silently outside this window. e.g. start=8, end=21 means run 8am through 8:59pm.
sec to sec
After each cycle, a random delay in this range is picked before the next one runs. 1200–3000 = 20–50 min.
